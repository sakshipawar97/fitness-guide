Goal: Improve overall strength and endurance.

Duration: 30 minutes

Frequency: 3 times a week

Squats

3 sets of 12 reps

Instructions:

  • Stand with feet shoulder-width apart.
  • Lower your body by bending your knees until thighs are parallel to the ground.
  • Keep your back straight and chest up.
  • Push through your heels to return to standing position.

Push-Ups

3 sets of 10 reps

Instructions:

  • Place hands shoulder-width apart on the floor.
  • Lower yourself until your chest nearly touches the ground.
  • Keep your body straight and core tight.
  • Push yourself back up to the starting position.

Plank

3 sets of 15 seconds

Instructions:

  • Lie face down and lift your body on your forearms and toes.
  • Keep your back straight and core engaged.
  • Hold this position without raising or dropping your hips.
