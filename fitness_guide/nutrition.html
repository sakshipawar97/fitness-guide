🥗 Nutrition Tips

Follow a balanced diet for the best fitness results.

🍗 High Protein Intake

Eat lean proteins like chicken, eggs, tofu, and lentils to build muscle.

💧 Stay Hydrated

Drink at least 2-3 liters of water daily to keep your body energized.

🍏 Fresh Fruits & Veggies

Include a variety of fruits and vegetables for vitamins and fiber.

🥑 Healthy Fats

Consume avocados, nuts, and olive oil for essential fatty acids.

🍚 Balanced Carbs

Opt for whole grains like oats, quinoa, and brown rice for sustained energy.

🚫 Limit Sugar & Processed Foods

Avoid sugary drinks, fast food, and refined carbs to stay fit.

🕒 Eat at the Right Time

Have small meals every 3-4 hours to maintain energy levels.

🛌 Get Enough Sleep

Aim for 7-9 hours of sleep to help your body recover and grow.
