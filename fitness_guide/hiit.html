Goal: Burn fat and improve endurance.

Duration: 20 minutes

Frequency: 4 times a week

Jump Rope

5 minutes continuous

Instructions:

  • Hold the jump rope handles and rotate the rope using your wrists.
  • Jump slightly off the ground as the rope passes under your feet.
  • Maintain a steady pace and keep your knees slightly bent.

Burpees

3 sets of 15 reps

Instructions:

  • Start in a standing position.
  • Drop into a squat and place your hands on the floor.
  • Kick your legs back into a push-up position.
  • Jump back to a squat and explode upwards.

Mountain Climbers

3 sets of 30 seconds

Instructions:

  • Get into a push-up position with your hands directly under your shoulders.
  • Quickly drive your knees toward your chest one at a time.
  • Maintain a fast pace while keeping your core tight.
