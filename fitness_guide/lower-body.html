Goal: Strengthen legs and improve mobility.

Duration: 25 minutes

Frequency: Twice a week

Lunges

3 sets of 12 reps

Instructions:

  • Stand upright and take a step forward with one leg.
  • Lower your body until your back knee nearly touches the ground.
  • Push through your front foot to return to the starting position.

Sumo Squats

3 sets of 12 reps

Instructions:

  • Stand with feet wider than shoulder-width apart, toes pointed outward.
  • Lower your hips until your thighs are parallel to the ground.
  • Push back up while keeping your chest lifted.

Calf Raises

3 sets of 10 reps

Instructions:

  • Stand upright with feet hip-width apart.
  • Lift your heels off the ground and balance on your toes.
  • Slowly lower yourself back to the starting position.
